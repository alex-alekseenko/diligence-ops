Built for Clarity

Every feature is designed to make financial data more readable, more beautiful, and more actionable.

01
Sankey Flow Charts
Trace every dollar from revenue sources through cost centers to net profit. Interactive nodes reveal breakdowns on hover.
02
Revenue Trend Lines
SVG-drawn trend lines with peak annotations and animated data points. Bar overlays show expense comparisons.
03
Stacked Breakdowns
Quarterly and segment breakdowns with animated stacked bars. Percentage labels embedded directly in segments.
04
Ticker Strips
Five-metric ticker strips with delta indicators. Color-coded for revenue, profit, and cost at a glance.
05
Dark Mode
Warm dark theme preserving the editorial aesthetic. Every token adapts — backgrounds, borders, data colors, and shadows.
06
Accessibility
WCAG AA compliant. Keyboard navigation for Sankey nodes, prefers-reduced-motion support, colorblind-safe patterns.
